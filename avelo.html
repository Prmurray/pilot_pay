AVELO AIRLINES

QUICKTAKE
  • Bolstering pilot compensation on Feb 1 2022, elevating 1st year CA pay by nearly 50% and FO pay by nearly 30%
  • Offering $20,000 signing bonus to new pilots who accept job offer before June 1, 2022. Initial $5000 paid after pilot completes OE and $15,000 paid at first year anniversary. Bonus available to both FOs and Direct Entry Captains.
  • Attend the pilot virtual information session on Monday 24 Jan at 1600 EST on Microsoft teams. Pilot Virtual Meeting. Click here to join the meeting.
  • Training pay is now $6300/month based on 70 hour FO guarantee beginning on first day of training
  • Avelo remains the only airline to pay an additional $1800 a month to every pilot to help offset the cost of commuting, even if pilot chooses to live in base
  • •Founder and CEO Andrew Levy was cofounder and former president of Allegiant Air and former CFO at United Airlines
  • Our pilots lead by living our values of Safety Always, Keeping it Simple, Do The Right Thing, and One Crew on every flight
  • Company website www.aveloair.com
  • Hiring 20 pilots for classes every month indefinitely
  • Line bidding for schedules
  • Fleet currently 6 737NG’s, adding 8-10 aircraft per year (40 in 5 years)
  • BUR and HVN current bases, adding 2-3 additional bases each year
  • New pay scale as of Feb 2022
  • Insurance options include Medical,Vision, Dental, Long Term and Short Term disability
  • Route map: www.aveloair.com/destinations
  • CASS and KCM participant
  • ATC callsign “Avelo”
  • IATA code “XP”; ICAO code “VXP”
  • Page updated on January 24, 2022 (Hiring)
