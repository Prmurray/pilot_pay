Allegiant Air

Las Vegas, NV

Company Website

QUICKTAKE
  • Boeing’s first U.S. ULCC Customer - Agreement with Boeing for 50 new 737 MAX family aircraft with option of 50 more – B737-7 and B737-8-200
  • First delivery in the Fall of 2023, continuing through 2024 and 2025
  • Growing fleet by 14% in 2021 – planning 153 aircraft by 2024 (108 EOY 2021)
  • Planning to hire pilots fall 2021
  • Austin domicile opening November 2021
  • Des Moines base now opening July 2021
  • Appleton base will open March 2022
  • Flint base will open February 2022
  • Operating 500+ routes to 120+ destinations across the U.S. and Caribbean
  • Nearly all trips are day turns consisting of 2, 3, or 4 legs 
  • Duty times range from 4 hours to 117 duty limits
  • Secured stadium naming rights with Raiders NFL team
  • NFL partnership allows access to games and special in-game and season-long promotions for NFL fans across the country
  • Official airline partner of the Vegas Golden Knights NHL team and T-Mobile Arena
  • Official airline of MiLB • First official airline partner of NASCAR
  • Official airline of the NCAA PAC-12 Conference 
  • First U.S. produced A320 delivered to Allegiant
  • Started service to Key West 
  • Announced service to Portland (PDX) starting May 2021
  • Announced service to Phoenix Sky Harbor (PHX), Minneapolis (MSP), Washington Dulles (IAD).  
  • Announced service to Boston Logan, Chicago Midway and Houston Hobby starting May 2020 
  • Service to Anchorage, Alaska starting May 2019
  • Orlando (SFB) is now the company’s largest base
  • All pilots provided parking passes for their base, and for LAS airport; all parking fees are paid for, during training
  • IATA code: G4; ICAO code: AAY
  • ATC callsign: "Allegiant"
  • This page was last updated: January 31, 2022 (Quicktake, Fleet)

Is Hiring?: Yes

Hiring from July 2021 to February 2022 264 pilots 

What is the web site URL that pilots should visit for more information: https://www.allegiantair.jobs/careers/pilots/

Pilot Hiring Minimums: 

  • 1500+ flights hours (fixed wing) 

Allowances for military conversion (x1.3)

  • FAA issued Airline Transport Pilot Certificate

Airplane Multiengine Land (AMEL) 

  • Current FAA First Class Medical Certificate
  • Unrestricted ATP

Preferred Pilot Hiring Requirements:

• 3000 hours Total Time 

• 1000 hours’ multi-engine turbine/jet 

• 1000 hours PIC 

• 121 experience 

• Currency within the previous 12 Months 

• Bachelor's degree

Pilot Hiring Process, please describe: 

Link to application: First Officer Application

After applying, these are the next steps:

  • Video Interview
  • Behavioral Assessment
  • Interview with Regional Chief Pilots
